# DLI-LLM Annotation

## Full Title

Differentiable Latent Indexing in LLMs: Toward Native Retrieval as a Cognitive Function

## Canonical Role

Architectural foundation of the Latent Cognition research program.

## Core Claim

Retrieval should not remain an external procedural layer but may be modeled as a differentiable cognitive function embedded within the internal representational dynamics of LLM architectures.

## Relationship to the Trilogy

This paper supplies the architectural substrate for the Trilogy. The Trilogy develops broader cognitive, philosophical, and theoretical implications, while DLI-LLM defines the systems-level mechanism.
